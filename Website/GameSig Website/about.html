About Me

Designing UX is something I am passionate about. I always like to praise good UX while critiquing terrible ones by simply observing every little detail of the design and how it translates with people. In the words of Donald Norman, “design is really an act of communication.” That is something I consistently pursue.

Windows, and Mac are platforms I have worked with before. Balsamiq, Sketch, and Origami Studio are tools I use to create designs, and produce prototypes and wireframes. I have designed UI for mobile application and website application with functioning prototypes. In addition, I also have experience with researching and facilitating UX.
