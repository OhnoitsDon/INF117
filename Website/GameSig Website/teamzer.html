Teamzer Website

Teamzer is a website application that aims to help UCI professors build teams for their students, featuring a questionnaire for students to take that evaluates personality traits. The interface consists of clear and simple list of teams so that professors can easily look at and manage them. The questionnaire takes in the form of most popular online personality tests and makes the best of their format.

Low and high-fidelity mockups were made in conjuction with the back-end development of the website. Morevoer, the value of teamwork was heavily emphasized in this project.
