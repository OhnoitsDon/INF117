The Malmik Necklace

The Malmik Necklace is a paired device for two close people to easily stay in “touch.” Each partner can press a button on their necklace that will send their partner’s necklace a signal that initiates warming vibrations and vice versa. The necklaces are accompanied with a mobile app that connects to each necklace via bluetooth. From the app, users can track metrics with their partners such as their heart rate, distance, and buzz frequency.

A system requirements document was genreated, detailing the team's goals and purposes, use cases description, use case diagrams, storyboards, etc. In addition, competitive analysis was used to evaluate how a given product’s competition stacks up against usability and design standards. It essentially helps to understand the playing field and how competitors are doing it.
