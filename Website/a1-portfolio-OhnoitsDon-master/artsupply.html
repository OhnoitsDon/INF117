Art Supply Warehouse Redesign

Art Supply Warehouse (ASW) is a store that sells arts and crafts supplies. The website that they have employed is meant to be an online interface that their customers can use to buy supplies from anywhere.

In the redesign, we improved the UX by cleaning the UI, adding new potential features while deleting other ones. Before facilitating the UX of the wesbsite, it was necessary to perform user research, usability testing, and other expert methods to identify and resolve the usability issues found.
